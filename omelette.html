Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Cheese (grated)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk until well combined.
  2. Add milk, salt, and pepper, then whisk again.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour in the egg mixture.
  4. Sprinkle the grated cheese on top once the eggs begin to set.
  5. Fold the omelette in half and cook until the cheese melts.
  6. Slide onto a plate and serve hot.
